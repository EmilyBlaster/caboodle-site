the design

four features. one workflow.

Built from scratch with Claude Code. Every feature maps directly to a real friction point in the stakeholder feedback process.

01

inline pins

Click anywhere on the page to leave a comment. No screenshots, no email, no "where exactly?" confusion.

02

color-coded reviewers

Each stakeholder gets a color. Instantly see who said what, without reading every comment.

03

threaded side panel

All feedback lives in a slide-out panel, grouped by section. Scroll through everything without losing context.

04

resolve tracking

Mark feedback resolved with a click. Visual confirmation it's been addressed — no follow-up email required.

stakeholder feedback loops are one of the biggest drags on any learning project. email chains, annotated pdfs, screenshots of screenshots. the real problem isn't that stakeholders give bad feedback — it's that the feedback process creates noise instead of signal.
